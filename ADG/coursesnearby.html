What level of disc golfer are you?

1. Austin Ridge Bible Church DGC - A very arduous course hiking wise, Bible Ridge provides great views as well as many challenging holes over water with many trees and rocks

2. Zilker Park - A great beginner course, Zilker is an all par 3 course that provides spectacular sunset views and a great time with friends.

3. Roy G. Guerrero DGC - Roy G., or Roy tree as it is sometimes called, is a masterpiece of a course. However, it is not for beginners so make sure you know your stuff before coming here.

4. Met Center - Met Center is a very difficult course, especially from the long tees on a windy day.

5. East Metro Park- Another very challenging course in East Austin.

6. Searight Park - Searight is a good beginner course like Zilker. Just make sure you time your round right to avoid large crowds.

7. Circle C Metro Park - Circle C is another exceptional Austin course that is great for intermediate players. Make sure to stay in the fairway or you'll be taking big numbers.

8. Northtown Park - Up in Pflugerville, Northtown is another great intermediate course that provides a wide variety of shots and hole difficulty levels.

9. Wells Branch DGC - Located right next to Northtown, Wells Branch is a great course for beginners who want to work their way up to Northtown

10. Bartholomew Park DGC - A nifty 15 hole course in East Austin, Bartholomew is great for forehand players or backhand players who love a challenge. Make sure you go in the winter when the grass is short.
